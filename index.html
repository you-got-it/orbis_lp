What is ‘I’d see’?

A platform for people to share their favourite moments to raise awareness about avoidable blindness.

who are orbis?

We are an international eye care charity that saves people’s sight, giving them the chance to change their lives. We train eye health professionals and workers, raise awareness, and improve access to eye care around the world.
