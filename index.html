If you lost your sight, what moment would you see again?

Globally, there are 43 million people living with blindness and 295 million people living with moderate-to-severe sight loss. 3 out of 4 of these people are completely treatable.

What is ‘I’d see’?

A platform for people to share their favourite moments to raise awareness about avoidable blindness.

share your memory

Losing your sight is emotionally traumatic and isolating. We are collecting these memories to share on a public website for others to read, to bring us closer together and better understand the impact vision loss can have on children and adults in low-income countries.

who are orbis?

We are an international eye care charity that saves people’s sight, giving them the chance to change their lives. We train eye health professionals and workers, raise awareness, and improve access to eye care around the world.
